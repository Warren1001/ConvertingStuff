1. Introduction
The Kicksin Assassin is known for high single target damage and decent mobility. She is one of the strongest Uber killing characters in Diablo 2 and can be used in combination with items that proc various skills to clear out a lot of PvE content. Overall she is not super gear intensive but does require a couple of solid key items to really get going.

* Dragon Claw - 1 Point - Prerequisite to Dragon Flight, required
