1. Introduction
The Wind Druid is a relatively strong and safe build that can clear out a large portion of the zones in Diablo 2 without much fear. He does not require a ton of gear but getting a couple of key items can really help accelerate his farming speed and keep him safe from harm.
2. Quick Reference
2.1. Skill Build
* Hurricane - 20 Points - Main crowd control and cold damage dealer, required
* Tornado - 20 Points - Main damage dealer, required
* Twister - 20 Points - Prerequisite to Tornado, required
* Cyclone Armor - 20 Points - Great Elemental Absorb, required
* Arctic Blast - 1 Point - Prerequisite to Tornado, required
* Summon Grizzly - 1+ Points - Great Tank, optional
* Summon Dire Wolf - 1 Point - Prerequisite to Summon Grizzly, optional
* Summon Spirit Wolf - 1 Point - Prerequisite to Summon Grizzly, optional
* Raven  - 1 Point - Prerequisite to Summon Grizzly, optional
* Oak Sage - 1-20 Points - Bonus to life for you and party, required

2.3. Gear
Damage Build
* Weapon: Heart of the Oak, Suicide Branch
* Weapon Swap: CTA + Spirit
* Shield: 35% Spirit Monarch
* Body Armor: Enigma
* Helm: Tornado Pelt, Druid FCR Circlet
* Gloves: Trangs or Magefist

* Belt: Arachnid Mesh
* Ring 1: Bul Kathos or 10 FCR Rings if wanting to hit 163 FCR Breakpoint

* Amulet: Mara’s Kaleidoscope or Crafted FCR Amulet for 163 FCR Breakpoint

* Charm 2: Annihilus Charm
* Charm 3: 9x Skiller GCs w/ Life or FHR to hit 63% or 99% FHR
* Charm 4: 10 small charms with life, res, mana, fhr depending on needs

Budget Mercenary Gear
* Weapon: Obedience
* Body Armor: Treachery
* Helm: Vampire Gaze
