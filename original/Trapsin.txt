1. Introduction
The “Trapsin” Assassin is a crowd controlling expert who utilizes a variety of ranged abilities to keep herself safe while her traps attack enemies. She is a great overall character with decent frames, stats, and damage. She is one of the safest and easiest characters to play in the game and is highly recommended for newer players.
2. Quick Reference
2.1. Skill Build
* Lightning Sentry - 20 points - Max immediately
* Death Sentry - 20 points - Max immediately after Lightning Sentry
* Shock Web - 20 points - Max Immediately after Death Sentry
* Charged Bolt Sentry - 20 points - Maxed immediately after Shock Web
* Fire Blast - 1+ point - prerequisite for Traps, Max after everything else or if not going for Shadow Master
* Mind Blast - 1 point invest when needing crowd control
* Burst of Speed - 1 point - invest immediately
* Fade - 1 point for resistances/damage reduction
* Psychic Hammer - 1 point - prerequisite for Mind Blast
* Cloak of Shadows - 1 point - prerequisite for Mind Blast + Good Crowd Control
* Claw Mastery - 1 point - prerequisite for Burst of Speed
* Shadow Warrior - 1 point - prerequisite for Shadow Master, optional
* Shadow Master - 1+ point - optional if you want an extra minion to help
