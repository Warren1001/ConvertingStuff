1. Introduction
The Bladesin is a quirky build of the Assassin that uses her rarely-used Blade Trap skills. These Traps are based on her physical damage so having a solid weapon is highly recommended. Overall this character struggles through the game but with high end gear can be playable.
2. Quick Reference
2.1. Skill Build
* Blade Fury - 20 Points - Main Damage Skill, required
* Blade Sentinel - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Blade Fury, required
* Wake of Fire - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Blade Fury, required
* Fire blast - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Blade Fury, required
* Blade Shield - 1 Point - Close up damage and protection, optional
* Venom - 20 Points - Increases overall damage, required
* Mind Blast - 1 Point - Conversion + Crowd Control, required
* Cloak of Shadows - 1 Point - Crowd Control, required
* Psychic Hammer - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Cloak of Shadows
* Fade - 1+ Points - Increased Resistances and Physical Damage Reduction, required
* Burst of Speed - 1 Point - Prerequisite to Fade, required
* Claw Mastery - 1+ Points - Prerequisite to Burst of Speed and if using Claws can put more points here, required
* Shadow Master - 20 Points - Pick this or Shadow Warrior, optional
* Shadow Warrior - 20 Points - Pick this or Shadow Master, optional
* Weapon Block - 1 Point - Prerequisite to Shadow Warrior, required
* Death Sentry - 20 Points - Useful for blowing up dead corpses and AOE, required
* Lightning Sentry - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Death Sentry, required
* Charged Bolt Sentry - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Death Sentry, required
* Shock Web - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Death Sentry, required
