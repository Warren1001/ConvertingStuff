1. Introduction
The Whirlwind Barbarian is a gear dependent character that is often used in PvP but can still have decent use in PvE. It is one of the most iconic builds in Diablo 2 and while it used to be one of the strongest builds in the game in earlier patches, it has been nerfed some but still has viability.
2. Quick Reference
2.1. Skill Build
* Whirlwind - 20 Points - Main damage dealer, required
* Leap Attack - 1 Point - Useful for movement, required
* Leap - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Leap Attack, required
* Concentrate - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Whirlwind, required
* Stun - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Whirlwind, required
* Bash - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Whirlwind, required
* Weapon Mastery of Choice - 20 Points - Adds Damage, Attack Rating, and Critical Strike
* Increased Stamina - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Increased Speed, required
* Increased Speed - 1 Point - Helps you move faster, required
* Iron Skin - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Natural Resistance, required
* Natural Resistance - 1 Point - Massive boost to Resistances, required
* Battle Command - 1 Point - Increases Skills , required
* Battle Orders - 20 Points - Massive Life and Mana boosts, required
* Shout - 1+ Points - Increases Defenses, required
* Howl - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Battle Shout, required
* Taunt - 1 Point - Can be useful to draw out mobs, optional
* Battle Cry - 1+ Points - Great for lowering Damage and Defense of Monsters, optional
* Find Potion - 1 Point - Prerequisite for Find Item, optional
* Find Item - 1+ Points - Helpful if running a lot of magic find to find more items, optional
